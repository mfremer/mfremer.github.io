I am currently a temporary researcher and teaching assistant (ATER) at [Université de Lorraine](https://www.univ-lorraine.fr/en/univ-lorraine/) during the 2024-2025 academic year.

I did my PhD in the [MFX](https://mfx.loria.fr/team/) team at [Loria](https://www.loria.fr/en/) (Nancy, France), under the supervision of Sylvain Lefebvre on the topic of **Layout problems and generative design for shape modeling**. My thesis is available on [theses.fr](https://theses.fr/2024LORR0073).
